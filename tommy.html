"I'm like Dream but better."-Tommy is a pretty cool streamer on Twitch! He mainly plays minecraft, and his streams attract anywhere from 100-500 thousand viewers at a time. He tends to make content that is loud and more humor based than gameplay based. While he does focus on humor and entertainment, he does have a reputation for being good at PVP and fighting games. Tommy is also a member of the Dream SMP, and he often streams there with Wilbur, Phil, Tubbo, and many others.
